OMW Upload How To

This page provides documentation on how to upload a wordnet to the OMW:

Online

  1. Login to OMW by clicking on the user icon. Use the username and password that was assigned to you. If not, get in touch with Francis Bond to discuss your project.
  2. Go to the upload page. At the bottom of the page will be a Choose file option. Alternatively, submit a URL for OMW to download. Then, press validate to determine whether your wordnet is ready to be uploaded or not.
  3. Before validating the wordnet, be sure this is the wordnet you wish to upload! If so, press Upload Wordnet.

Local Changes

  1. To locally run the OMW, you need to setup OMW on your PC. Follow these steps for installation.
  2. After successfully installing OMW locally in your PC, login to OMW by clicking on the user icon . This will direct you to a login page, where the username is the admin (password is same that you entered while installing the OMW locally).
  3. Common mistakes that lead to the Validation Failed! message:



    Make sure your project is in the database! Click on Add New Project to fix this error. Your project name should be the same as the wordnet ID you specified in your XML file.

    Make sure your language is in the database! Click on Add New Language to fix this error.
